Inspiration

The group was interested in looking for a subject of study that was fun, not related to our jobs, that had an easy to manipulate dataset and that we all loved, so no other subject caught our interests as much as pets/animals in Austin. Austin is growing rapidly and has an increasing number of animals brought to shelters. Data ranges from 2014 to 2017. We chose to limit our information to cats and dogs as they formed the strong majority of animals in shelters.

We wanted to answer questions such as:

  • What kind of animals are in animal shelters?
  • What are the outcomes of an animalâ€™s visit?
  • How long do animals typically stay in shelters?
  • Where and what time are animals brought to the shelter?

Process

In order to best display data from Austin animal shelters, the group created a full-stack, interactive dashboard hosted on this website. Data is pulled in from an Austin animal shelter data set. The website uses the data to create at four visualizations using javascript and various third-party libraries. The visualizations are interactive. The website uses HTML code and bootstrap components to build the structure.
